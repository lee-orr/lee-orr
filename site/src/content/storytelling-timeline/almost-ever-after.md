From Andrew Seok (Rosamund, The Man With The Golden Heart) comes Almost Ever After -
five interconnected love stories brought to life with original songs inspired by The Beatles, Bruno Mars, Adele,
and more. A modern, musical journey through love in all its magic, mess, and meaning.
