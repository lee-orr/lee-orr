## Interactive Media

### Teddy & Drew - An Interactive Musical (2021)

An experiment in combining branching narrative mechanics with musical theatre. The musical is a reflection on an old relationship. Audience choices determine whether the relationship lasts or comes to an end.  Lee-Orr co-wrote the music, lyrics and script with Jackson Seib. She was also the voice of Drew.


### Crossroad Blues (2023)

A small game made over 10 days for VimJam 4.  The player takes the role of a lowly demon trying to reach the crossroads in time to make a deal and earn a soul. Lee-Orr was responsible for all aspects of game development, including scoring each level with distinct music.

<Block>
    ## Writing for the Stage

### The Love We Know

A 10-minute musical telling the story of a relationship, from first meeting to break-up. The music was composed by Lee-Orr. The lyrics and book were co-written by Lee-Orr and Julian Lam.

### Script?

A time-bending 10-minute musical about a failed collaboration, and whether it is worth trying again. The music was composed by Lee-Orr, who was also the voice of Mik. The book and lyrics were co-written by Lee-Orr and Angelica Bolognesi Bonacini.

### Doctor Faustus Lights the Lights
A choral setting of a selection from Gertrude Stein’s “Doctor Faustus Lights the Lights”, composed and performed for a theatre history showcase class at Randolph College for the Performing Arts.

### Listen to William from Slay the Dragon Extended
*Slay the Dragon Extended* was a play written and directed by Brian De La Franier. Lee-Orr wrote and performed Listen to William for use in promotional materials and the curtain call.

## Online Video

### Narrative Animated Songs (2022)

Wrote, produced, performed and animated for release on YouTube, these narrative songs were an experiment in combining musical-theatre conventions, a storyboard-like animation style, and low-poly 3d graphics. Each song was composed, written and recorded by Lee-Orr, some with guest performers, before being animated and edited in the Unity game engine with the help of assets by Synty Studios.
